Services like social networks, web analytics, and intelligent e-commerce often need to manage data at a scale too big for a traditional database. Complexity increases with scale and demand, and handling Big Data is not as simple as just doubling down on your RDBMS or rolling out some trendy new technology.

Big Data systems use many machines working in parallel to store and process data,which introduces fundamental challenges unfamiliar to most developers.
